Introduction to Economy


Definition

The study on how the society use the limited resources to satisfy the unlimited wants


Resources

  1. Land - natural resources
  2. Labor - workers contributes physical and mental efforts
  3. Capital - man made product used to futher the production process (building, matertials, machines, tools)
  4. Entrepreneur - the person who combine all the resources and bear the risks to produce products

Concept

  1. Scarcity
  2. Choice
  3. Opportunity Cost


Economic Components

  1. Household - owner of the factor of production
    • land
    • labor
    • capital
    • entrepreneur

  2. Firm
    • will produces good and services
    • will used the input from household
    • payment need to be done for the input

    • labor: salary
    • machine: interest
    • land: rent
    • entrepreneur

  3. Government
    • charge taxes and provide subsidies to the certain producer
    • government also make payment if they need to use any resources from the household

  4. Rest of the World
    • International trade (import and export)

Economic Optimization

  1. Profit Maximization
    • TP = TR - TC
    • TP = (P X Q) - (TFC + TVC)

  2. Revenue Maximization
    • Price X Quantity
    • TR = P X Q

  3. Cost Minimization
    4. short run production
    • Fixed Cost
      • Rental for factory, land
    • Variable Cost(allow to change)
      • raw materials
      • labor
      • Electricity
    • TC = TFC + TVC
