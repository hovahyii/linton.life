How to get 50% discount while riding MRT, Rapid KL and LRT

MyRapid Concession Card provides 50% discount for student, senior citizens, and OKU. For your information, MyRapid is a online platform sharing or providing Malaysia's public transport systems and services under Prasarana Malaysia Berhad. Kindly visit https://www.myrapid.com.my/corporate-information/who-we-are

How to apply for MyRapid Concession Card

First of all, you have to visit the official website: https://www.myrapid.com.my/traveling-with-us/deals-promotions/concession-cards-for-students-senior-citizen-and-oku.

Then click 'Click here to apply now!'

You will be directed to the webpage whereby you need to register your account and login. After you done and successfully logged in, you will see the page like this.

You should click 'Concession Card' Then, you will be redirect to fill a form for concession card. Fill the details and documents as stated by the website.

Once you fill in the form and attach all the documents needed. You should wait for the email to reply, it might took longer than weeks. When you received an email from MyRapid, you can retrieve your MyRapid Concession Card at the place you chosen, which are:

  1. Hang Tuah LRT Station
  2. Bandar Tun Hussien Onn MRT Station
  3. SunU- Monash BRT Station
  4. Pasar Seni Concession Card Counter (located at Pasar Seni Bus Hub)

For more information about the MyRapid Concession Card, you may visit: https://www.myrapid.com.my/clients/Myrapid_Prasarana_37CB56E7-2301-4302-9B98-DFC127DD17E9/contentms/img/pdf/FAQ_Cashless_MRTFB_28012019.pdf

Important

You need to get your confirmation letter by requesting it at Student Affairs. The letter usually takes 3-4 days to process.
