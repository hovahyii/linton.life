How to go to Mid Valley or Bukit Bintang from Linton University College

How to go to Mid Valley or Bukit Bintang from Linton University College

If you just arrived at Linton University College and want to go to Mid Valley or Bukit Bintang but don't know how. Don't worry. I am about to teach you the cheapest way.

To go to Mid Valley or Bukit Bintang, you must first go to KTM at Batang Benar.

If you are going to there during day time or night time, you can always call MyCar, usually fees will be around RM 10 to RM 12 depends on the time. Or you can always follow Linton University College bus to there:

Day Time
Monday, Friday     5:30 PM
Saturday       11:00 AM or 11:30 AM
Sunday       11:00 AM or 11:30 AM

At KTM Batang Benar counter, you need to buy a KTM card, you can select the cheapest price which is RM 13. Then walk to the other side using the crossover bridge. You should search for Kajang on the monitor board or just ask someone.

KTM usually takes 30 minutes or longer to arrive, depends on your luck that day. There is no schedule or timer to tell you when does the KTM come.

To Mid Valley

If you are planning to go to Mid Valley, you can just stay on the KTM. It will reach there in 40 minutes.

To Bukit Bintang

There are few stations or routes you can use to go there. I just share my way only.

After you get on KTM, you wait for 2 stations to pass, which are Bangi and UKM. Once you arrived at Kajang station, you get down from KTM and change to MRT.

At the MRT, you might need to use Touch and Go card or your MyRapid Concession card. If your IC is new and labelled with Touch and Go icon at the back, you can top up your credits and use it for MRT.

MRT has a fixed schedule and you can see the arrival of the MRT by referring to the monitor screen.

Get on the MRT and it will bring you to Bukit Bintang.
